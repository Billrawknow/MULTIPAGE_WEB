Our Facilities

KPLU boasts a comprehensive sports infrastructure catering to both recreational and competitive needs, well-maintained for use by students, staff, and the community. Key facilities include:
1. Swimming Pool (Main Campus): Positioned opposite the tennis courts, this facility is exclusive to students and staff members, along with their dependents. It operates Monday through Friday from 8.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m, and on Saturdays and Sundays from 9.00 a.m. to 3.00 p.m. The charges are 20/- for students and 50/- for staff.
2. Soccer Pitches: The university houses four soccer pitches, with three located at the upper fields and one at the lower ground.
3. Volleyball Courts: There are five volleyball courts, all situated at the lower ground.
4. Netball Pitches: Two netball pitches are available, located at the lower ground.
5. Handball Pitches: The university features four handball pitches at the upper ground.
6. Rugby Pitch: A single rugby pitch is located at the upper field.
7. Hockey Fields: Two murram hockey fields are present, one opposite the competition center and another at the lower grounds.
8. Basketball Courts: There are three basketball courts situated at Bishop Square.
9. Athletics Tracks: The university offers two athletics tracks, one at the upper ground and another at the lower ground.
10. Tennis Courts: Four tennis courts are available, located opposite the lower grounds near the Directorate office.
